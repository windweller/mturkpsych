Welcome to our Experiment!

This study is for native English speakers that are currently in the United States. Please no not accept this HIT if you are not a native English speaker or are not currently living in the United States.

You have been invited to take part in a research study by the Mind and Langauge Lab at Emory University. This study investigates semantic relationships between verbs.

This study consists of three parts: a practice session, an experimental session, and a brief demographic questionnaire. During the practice an experimental sessions, you will be asked to make judgments based on the meaning of verbs.

Though the actual time will vary from person to person, on average the task takes 10-15 minutes to complete.

After completing ALL three parts, you will be compensated with $0.75.
