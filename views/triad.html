Welcome to our Experiment!

In this window, you will complete the first and second parts of the HIT. You will be asked to complete practice trails and then experimental trials.

The task is simple. You will first see a single verb on the screen. After a brief delay, two more verbs will appear on the screen below the first verb. Your task will be to decide which of the two verbs on the bottom is most similar in meaning to the verb on the top.

A

B         C

For example, if the verb "A" appears first and is followed by the verbs "B" and "C" you will have to decide if the meaning of "B" or "C" is more similar to the meaning of "A". If you think that the verb on the left ("B" in this example) is most similar in meaning to the verb on the top ("A" in this example), you should press the LEFT ARROW key. If you think that the verb on the right ("C" in this example) is most similar, you should press the RIGHT ARROW key.

After pressing either the LEFT or the RIGHT arrow, you will be shown another set of three words. There will 10 practice trials and 100 experimental trials. You will be prompted when the practice trials are over and the experimental trials begin.

Please respond as quickly and as accurately as possible.

After the completion of the experimental round, you will be given a Qualtrics authentication code to use to complete the third part of our HIT.
