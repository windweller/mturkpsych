Here we extract future thinking from text. To run, paste text into the textbox and click the "Run Analysis" button. Results will appear on the bottom of the page. You can also be downloaded by pressing the "Download CSV" button.
Past: only past orientation
Present: neither past nor future orientation
Future: any future orientation, possibly future of past
