Welcome to our Experiment!

Hi! Welcome to our experiment. We are a research team in the Psychology department of Emory University trying to extract sections from a set of documents. Since these documents are too large and ungainly to be handled using programs, we must require human operators to extract the sections. Indeed, what we have at hand is more of a task than an experiment. In this experiment you will have ten random documents to complete in total. The entire experiment should take at most only 10-15 minutes minutes and it can be repeated multiple times, but each time will deal with a new set of documents.

The document that you need to extract information from can be downloaded as a file (and open in your browser), or you can choose to view it directly. Don't worry, we will guide you through the whole process. In order for your experience to be as smooth as possible, it's recommended that you install Firefox browser . Please DO NOT OPEN it in Internet Explorer.

We track your progress using Cookies. If you are using private browsing option, a public computer, or chose to clear your Cookies, your progress will be lost. The document is HUGE (on average 10-15 megabytes)! Please be sure to close any unnecessary tabs to improve browser performance.
