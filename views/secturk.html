Instruction:

The documents you will be working with are called 10-K reports, which are filed annually by specific companies to the SEC (Stock Exchange Committee). Each document has a fixed structure but differs in content. Your task is to extract one section out of each of these documents, and then copy and paste the content of these sections to this webpage. Only once the section has been posted will you be allowed continue on to the next document.

You will click the link below, which will open a new webpage that contains the file. Downloading will be slow because some files are very large (20mb - 30mb). We encourage you to use the Firefox browser .
