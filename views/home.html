Welcome to our Experiment!

Hi! Welcome to this experiment. We are a research team in Psychology department of Emory. This is more of a task than an experiment. We want to extract three sections from a large unorganized document, and it's very hard to do using programs, so we decide to ask for your help. You will have ten documents to complete in total. It might take 10-15 minutes minutes. If you are quick, it will be even faster for you. This experiment can be completed repeatedly, but you will deal with different documents.

The document you that you need to extract information from can be downloaded as a file (and open in your browser), or you can choose to view it directly. Don't worry, we will guide you through the whole process. In order for your experience to be as smooth as possible, it's recommended that you install Firefox browser . Please DO NOT OPEN it in Internet Explorer.

We track your progress using COOKIE. If you are using private browsing option, a public computer, or chose to clear your Cookie, your progress will be lost. The document is HUGE (on average 10-15 megabytes)! Please close any unnecessary tab so your experience is better.
